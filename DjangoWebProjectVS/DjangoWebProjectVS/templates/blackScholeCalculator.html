BlackSchole formula

Use the well-known formula to calculate vanilla call and put options: \begin{align} Call = & S N(d_1) - K e^{-r t} N(d_2) \\ Put = & K e^{-r t} N(-d_2) - N(-d_1) S \end{align} where $$ d_1 = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{t} } [ \ln(\frac{S}{K}) + (r-\frac{\sigma^2}{2}t) ] $$ and $$ d_2 = d_1 - \sigma \sqrt{t} $$
